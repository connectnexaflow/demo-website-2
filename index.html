About the Doctor

A Life Devoted to
Healing Naturally

Dr. Jay Kumar Verma is a renowned homeopathic physician with over 16 years of experience. He graduated in BHMS from Pt. Jawahar Lal Nehru Homeopathic Medical College, Kanpur, and went on to earn his PG Hom from Hahnemann College of Homeopathy, London — followed by Fellowship (FHCH-UK) and MD from Tantia University.

During his training he contributed to epidemic prevention during the 2008 Chikungunya outbreak in Kanpur. Today he runs two multispeciality clinics in Lucknow — in Aliganj and Kalyanpur — serving patients from across Uttar Pradesh and beyond.
